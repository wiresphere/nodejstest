Chans
This is something I made to fetch resources for me. I would like to do some digital art in the future and this is a great resource for me to harness

Features:
  • Cron runs to fetch new thumbnail from live Wallpaper general board (4chan)
  • I decide which threads I want to keep
  • Cron fetches full sized images of approved pictures

Cyanide & Happiness
Cyanide & Happiness is a long running web comic site. I was curious as to which of these comics was the most liked.

If you're curious; This is their most liked comic

  • jQgrid allows me to search by most popular across all time, or any period I want
